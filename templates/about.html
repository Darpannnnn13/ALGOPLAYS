Our Quest

Welcome to AlgoPlay, where the thrill of gaming meets the power of learning. Our mission is to demystify the complex world of algorithms by transforming them into engaging, interactive puzzles. We believe that the best way to learn is by doing, and the most fun way to do is by playing.

Meet the Algorithms

Pathfinding Algorithms (BFS, DFS, A*)

These are the explorers of our universe, used in games like Maze Solver and Pattern Lock. They are experts at navigating from a starting point to a goal, finding the shortest or best possible route. From GPS navigation to game character AI, pathfinding is everywhere.

Heuristic & Greedy Algorithms

The strategists. Found in games like Puzzle-8 and Sudoku, these algorithms make smart, educated guesses to find a solution quickly. They don't always guarantee the *best* path, but they are incredibly efficient at finding a great one.

Logic & Constraint Solvers

The master detectives. Games like Sudoku and Crossword rely on these algorithms to work through problems with a strict set of rules. They systematically eliminate possibilities until the one correct solution is found.
